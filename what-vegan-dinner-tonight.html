People always ask: 'What vegan dinner should I make tonight?' Well, you have a question and we have the answer!

These 7 recipes are delicious and perfect for anyone!

1. 15-Minute Stir-Fry

Quick and easy stir-fry with mixed fresh veggies and your favorite sauce. Perfect for busy weeknights. Use ingredients like bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas, tofu or tempeh, garlic, and soy sauce.

  • Chop all vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  • Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  • Add tofu or tempeh and cook until browned.
  • Add vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  • Pour soy sauce and your favorite stir-fry sauce, cook for another 2 minutes.
  • Serve hot with rice or noodles.

2. Vegan Mac & Cheese

Creamy, cheesy comfort food made with cashews or a store-bought vegan cheese sauce, pasta, and nutritional yeast for that cheesy flavor.

3. Buddha Bowl

A nutritious bowl with quinoa or brown rice, roasted veggies, leafy greens, chickpeas or beans, and a tahini or lemon dressing.

4. Vegan Lasagna

Layers of pasta sheets, tomato sauce, sautéed spinach, mushrooms, vegan ricotta (tofu or cashew-based), and dairy-free cheese.

5. Chickpea Curry

Hearty chickpeas cooked in a spiced tomato and coconut milk sauce, served with basmati rice or naan bread.

  • Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in oil.
  • Add spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder.
  • Stir in tomatoes and coconut milk, simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Add chickpeas and cook for 10-15 minutes until thickened.
  • Serve with rice or naan.

6. Stuffed Bell Peppers

Bell peppers filled with a savory mixture of quinoa, black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, and spices, baked until tender.

7. Lentil Tacos

Spiced lentils cooked with onions and peppers, served in corn tortillas with avocado, salsa, and fresh cilantro.
