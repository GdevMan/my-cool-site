Harry Styles and Zoë Kravitz Spark Dating Rumors After Romantic NYC Outing

Published: September 6, 2025

Source: Times of India – ETimes

Pop superstar Harry Styles and actress Zoë Kravitz have ignited a flurry of dating rumors after being spotted walking hand-in-hand through New York City earlier this week. The duo were seen casually dressed, sharing laughs and appearing extremely comfortable in each other’s company as they strolled through SoHo.

Photos of the two quickly made their way across social media, sparking a viral conversation that has dominated platforms like Twitter, TikTok, and Instagram. Fans are calling them a “power couple in the making,” and the internet has exploded with speculation, reaction memes, and celebrity gossip threads.

A History of Friendship – or Something More?

While this is the first time the pair have been seen together in a clearly romantic context, insiders say Styles and Kravitz have run in the same social circles for years. Both are connected to the fashion world, major film and music industry players, and are frequent guests at Met Galas, fashion shows, and private celebrity events.

Fans have been quick to dig up past interviews and red carpet clips where the two shared brief, friendly interactions. One fan tweeted, “We should’ve known this was coming — the chemistry was always there!”

Silence from Both Camps

Neither Styles nor Kravitz has made any public statement about the photos or the nature of their relationship. Representatives for both celebrities have declined to comment, only fueling speculation further. In the age of social media, silence speaks volumes.

Fans React — and Go Wild

On X (formerly Twitter), hashtags like #HarryAndZoe and #PowerCouple were trending globally within hours. Reactions ranged from excitement to heartbreak, with fans of both stars expressing their shock and glee over the unexpected pairing.

“Zoë Kravitz and Harry Styles together??? The aesthetic. The vibes. The LEWKS. I’m here for it,” wrote one user.

Others were more skeptical, questioning whether the outing was a PR stunt or simply two friends being misinterpreted. Still, the photos tell a different story — and the internet is convinced.

The Verdict

Whether it’s the start of a real relationship or just a moment of affection between friends, one thing’s for sure: Harry Styles and Zoë Kravitz know how to get the world talking. Until an official statement comes, fans will be left guessing — and clicking.
