Meet Mexico’s Xin Xin, the last panda on earth NOT owned by China

Xin Xin is the world’s only giant panda not owned by China and the last of her kind in Mexico. Born at Mexico City’s Chapultepec Zoo on July 1, 1990, she has reached 35 years of age — equivalent to more than 100 human years — and is among the oldest pandas alive.
